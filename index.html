About Technologent Engineering

Founded in 2020 and headquartered in Lahore, Technologent Engineering was established with a vision to deliver world-class industrial engineering and renewable energy solutions throughout Pakistan. Since inception, we have focused on precision engineering, regulatory compliance, safety excellence, and long-term operational efficiency. Our expertise spans large-scale industrial solar installations, EPC project management, electrical infrastructure systems, mechanical engineering works, and HVAC solutions tailored for manufacturing and heavy industry.
